Long Stay & Monthly Rates

All our rooms and the cottage are available for extended stays. If you're planning a longer visit, whether a week, a month, or more, get in touch directly and we'll work out a rate that makes sense. Direct bookings always get the best deal.

Notes & Policies

  • Children under 6 stay free
  • Children 6–12: 50% of adult rate
  • Full Breakfast (optional add-on): KES 1,200/adult · KES 600/child
  • H/B includes 3-course meal, exclusive of drinks
  • 50% deposit required to confirm booking
  • Minimum 2 nights: festive week & long weekends
  • Cancellation: Full refund if 72hrs+ before stay. No refund within 48hrs.
  • Additional guests: KES 4,000/adult/night (max 3 in Penthouse)
