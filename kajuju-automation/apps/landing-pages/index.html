Idan Barn Suites & Café — Mt Kenya

We're located in Naromoru, 1km off the Nanyuki highway. We offer boutique modern style rooms, a self-contained cottage, and an on-site café, for solo travellers, couples, families, and groups or Mt Kenya climbers and hiker enthusiasts. We're solar powered with no power outages, have fast Starlink WiFi all set on a quiet property with stunning Mt Kenya views when she's not hiding.

Mau Mau Caves hike

An 8–13km trek through forest and history, with the option to drive up to the waterfall section. A local guide will meet you at the start point.

Meet your guide at Trout Tree restaurant. Resident, non-resident, security, and guide fees apply.

E-bike rides

In partnership with Paiskeli, explore the Naromoru area on electric bikes. Rides can depart from Idan as your start point, or you can join group rides departing from Nanyuki. A guide is optional and recommended for first-timers.

Full day and half day options available. Guide optional. Use code IdanBarn when booking with Paiskeli.

Horse riding

In partnership with a local neighbour, horse rides are available approximately 10 minutes from Idan. A great way to explore the Naromoru landscape at a slower pace.

Inquire at reception for pricing and availability.

Nature walks

Trails around the property and the surrounding Naromoru area. Ask at check-in for current route recommendations and conditions.

Complimentary for guests.

Mt Kenya — climb and day hikes

We're located 19km from the Naromoru gate into Mt Kenya National Park, and approximately 1 hour from Sirimon gate, giving you flexibility on your chosen route. Whether you're going for the summit or a day hike into the mountain, we can help coordinate local cab transfers and connect you with registered guides for whichever route you choose.

See Mt Kenya Climb Package for full details.

Prices for guided activities, park entry, e-bike hire, and horse riding are estimates and subject to change. Always confirm current rates with the entrance gate, your guide, or the activity provider directly before your visit.

Planning a Mt Kenya climb or day hike? We're 19km from the Naromoru gate and approximately 1 hour from Sirimon gate. We can help coordinate guides and transfers. See our Mt Kenya Climb Package for details.
