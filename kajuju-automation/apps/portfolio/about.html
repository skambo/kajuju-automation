About

In the span of my career, I've tested systems that move money, mobile wallets, PSP integrations, lending and savings platforms, corporate card transactions, across markets in Africa and Europe including Saas and design platforms.

In 2025, after building, Kajuju, a boutique lodge in Kenya, I knew I’d spend time learning hospitality and customer service, however, I found myself relearning systems engineering. Bookings, payments, inventory, and guest communication all touch each other constantly, and realising most of the failures I encountered lived in the handoffs between them and rigid workflows built that did not reflect the reality of our in-house workflows.

Today I split my time between QA and automation engineering work and operating Kajuju, where I built and maintain the framework that runs it. Alongside this, I'm actively expanding my skills in AI-assisted software development and AI-driven test automation, using modern AI tools to accelerate test design, debugging, automation development, and day-to-day engineering workflows. I'm also building practical automation projects, including Kajuju Automation, to explore how AI can improve software quality without replacing disciplined engineering practices.

I'm looking for the next place, fintech, SaaS, hospitality tech, or another systems heavy product where reliability, thoughtful automation, and preventing failures matter.
